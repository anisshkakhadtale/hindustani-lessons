Background

A practice rooted in
tradition and performance.

“Sur is not just about notes, it is about surrender.” – Pandit Jasraj

I am Anisshka, a vocalist trained in Hindustani classical music with years of active experience, including concerts and cultural events. My musical practice spans formal study of raga structures, tala cycles, and bandish composition alongside ensemble performance in Indian classical music settings.

I have been a member and informal leader of the Indian Classical Arts Society at UMass Amherst, where I organised and performed at cultural events. My background also includes mentoring fellow musicians and introducing the art form to students with no prior exposure.

This summer, I am opening lessons to students of all ages, msinly curious beginners who wish to develop a foundational practice with discipline and structure.
